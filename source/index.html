Selection procedure

1. Are you a good fit?

Take the assessment test here to evaluate whether you meet what this project expects from you.

2. Select a time-slot

Select here a time-slot to pick up the device, and install and configure the Fitrockr app with Paul.

3. Comply to the requirements

Broadly this entails that you fill in the surveys and synchronize the device timely. In the assessment test of step 1 and the session of step 2 the requirements will be further elaborated.

4. Book 45 credits to your Panl account!

If, and only if, your participation is satisfactory, then you are eligible for the credits. These will be transferred to your Panl account at the end of the study.

FAQ

General

What are the start and end dates of the study?

The study is scheduled for tba.

What operating systems do my devices need to have?

The most recent version of either Android or Apple iOS suffices.

What parameters will be tracked?

Your heart rate, heart rate variability, sleep, etc.

What happens with the data after the study finished?

We will store it for 10 years to ensure our results are reproducible by fellow-researchers. Of course we will work in line with GDPR, i.e. we anonymise personal data and make sure re-identification by combining anonymised data is impossible.
