Take the assessment test here to evaluate whether you meet what this project expects from you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Please check the FAQ below, watch the tutorials and follow the steps in the user manuals to set up your device before enrolling for the individual session.

General

What are the start and end dates of the study?

The study is scheduled for February, 2022. The specific date will be communicated to you as soon as possible, and not later than 01 February.

What operating systems do my devices need to have?

The most recent version of either Android or Apple iOS suffices.

What parameters will the device track?

Your heart rate, heart rate variability, sleep, etc.

What happens with the data after the study finished?

We will store it for 10 years to ensure our results are reproducible. Of course we will work in line with GDPR, i.e. we anonymise personal data and make sure re-identification by combining anonymised data is impossible. See also our privacy policy for more information on this matter.

Can I keep the device after the project?

No, the device is bought by the Vrije Universiteit and you are obliged to return it within two weeks after the project. You can return it in the same room as the preliminary session, i.e. room 4A-44. The specific date will be communicated to you in the last week of the study. We will make sure this session is not in the week of the final exams.

Do I need to pay myself for any app or for the device?

No, participation is free, hence usage of the device and apps are free of charge. After the research project you will not be able to use the apps for personal purposes.
